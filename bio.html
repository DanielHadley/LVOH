Lisa Van Orman Hadley, writer

Stories Awards Home

Lisa lives in Cambridge, Massachusetts, with her husband, twins, and one-eyed cat. She graduated from the Warren Wilson M.F.A. Program for Writers and was a fellow at the Millay Colony for the Arts. She is currently working on a novel-in-stories, for which she received the Larry Levis Post-Graduate Fellowship and a Barbara Deming Memorial Fund Fellowship. Her stories have been published in Knee-Jerk, Opium, and elsewhere.
