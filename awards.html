The Larry Levis Post-Graduate Fellowship

The Larry Levis Post-Graduate Stipend is an award given to support a graduate of the Warren Wilson College MFA Program for Writers who is completing his/her first book. The Levis Stipend alternates between awards for poetry and for fiction



The Barbara Deming Memorial Fund Fellowship

Money for Women is the oldest ongoing feminist granting agency. After Barbara Deming‘s death in 1984, it became a memorial fund. The fund is in its third decade of giving encouragement and grants to individual feminists in the arts (writers, and visual artists). The Fund relies on a volunteer Board and judges to collaborate in making awards.
